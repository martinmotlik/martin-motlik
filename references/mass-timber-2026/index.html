International Mass Timber Conference 2026

Designed and prepared trade show booth #908 for Dlubal Software Inc. in collaboration with the US team at the International Mass Timber Conference, the world's largest gathering of mass timber experts, held at the Oregon Convention Center in Portland. The work covered graphic design of the booth concept, 3D renderings with full equipment layout, and production of print-ready files for the booth manufacturer.

About the event

The International Mass Timber Conference is the world's largest gathering of mass timber experts, covering the entire industry supply chain from design and engineering to manufacturing and construction. The program focuses on cross-laminated timber (CLT), glulam, nail-laminated timber, and other engineered wood products, bringing together architects, structural engineers, developers, and manufacturers from across North America and beyond.
